ABOUT BUTTERFLIES

Butterflies are found all around the world. There are around 20000 to 25000 species around the world. They are insects in the order of Lepidoptera. Lepidoptera comes from the Greek 'lepis' meaning the scale and 'pteron' meaning wings. The wing colors are made up of thousands of these small dusk-like scales.They are insects and their bodies are divided into 3 segments. Head, thorax, and abdomen. The head has eyes, antennae, and proboscis.[feeding tube or tongue]. The thorax has wings and legs. The abdomen has internal organs like the digestive, excretory, nervous, and sexual organs.

ABOUT BUTTER_LIFE ORGANISATION

Butter_life is an charitable organisation established in 2022. Its main is to raise awarenessof the decreasing number of butterflies around the world. It also encourages people to join the organisation and also take interest in butterflies and support butterfly population. Through our website, you will gain knowledge of about butterflies.
